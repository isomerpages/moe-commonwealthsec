<h4>9. Financial Assistance Schemes (FAS)</h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><u>MOE FAS</u>
</p>
<p>Singapore Citizen students who have financial needs can seek assistance
from MOE for school fees and other expenses if their monthly Gross Household
Income (GHI) is $4000 and below, or if their family's monthly Per Capital
Income (PCI) is $1000 or less.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Students whose 2026 MOE FAS application are already approved via your
Primary Schools or with siblings in other schools whose 2026 FAS application
are already approved, please email your approval letter to <a href="mailto:cwss@moe.edu.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">cwss@moe.edu.sg</a>.
We will prepare the FAS approval letter for you to collect books and uniforms
from our school bookshop.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>If you have not applied through your childâ€™s primary school for 2026 MOE
FAS, kindly do so immediately. You can access the online e-FAS application
form via the <strong>Parents Gateway App &gt;Services &gt; Financial Assistance </strong>or
via this link <a href="https://go.gov.sg/moe-efas" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/moe-efas</a>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><u>School Based FAS</u>
</p>
<p>Singapore Citizen students whose GHI is between $4,001 to $5,500, or PCI
is between $1,001 to $1,375 can apply for the school-based FAS. Please
contact the school at 6560 6866 or <a href="mailto:cwss@moe.edu.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">cwss@moe.edu.sg</a> if you meet the criteria.</p>
</li>
</ul>
